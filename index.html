If it goes sideways
CHECKPOINT 1 · ~1:00 PM, LONG BEACH

No legal street spot near Bluff Park after 20 minutes? Slide to the Belmont Shore / Granada zone — metered 2nd St parking, same synchronized show, mellower crowd.

CHECKPOINT 2 · HARD PIVOT BY 2:30 PM

If Long Beach feels wrong, drive to Dana Point: 405 S → 73 S (toll) → I-5 S, ~50–60 min. Street-park the Lantern District by 4 PM — you still get the 5:50 flyover and the 9 PM show from Lantern Bay Park. If DP jams too: Pines Park or Heritage Park still catch it all.

⚠ DON'T

Don't pivot to Huntington after 2 PM — $40 lots jammed, prime sand long gone, and the city itself warns of 90+ minute exits.
